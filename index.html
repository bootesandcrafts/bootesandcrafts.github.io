About Me

My name is Alana, I'm 5'7"... oh wait you probably want to know more about my life as a self proclaimed crafter! Well I've been crafting for as long as I can remember, something about making things with your hands has always drawn me to the craft life I use crafting as a way to break the cycle of boredom and allow myself to fall into a state of flow. My current interests are all over the map for the most part my cricut is what I jump to first for crafting, however I also enjoy making my own body products, knitting, latch hooking, and painting nails. Oh I almost forgot! If you're wondering who Boötes is... he's my cat! Follow along to see what projects I've been working on.
